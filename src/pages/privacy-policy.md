## What's collected

Non-personally identifiable data such as:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referring websites
- Browser and device information
- Country and city (based on anonymized IP address)

## Why it's collected

To understand how visitors interact with the pages, and to help identify and fix technical issues.

## Your rights

- Opt out of data collection
- Request information about your data
- Request correction or deletion of personal data

Since no personal identifiable data is collected, in most cases it won't be possible to link any data back to you.
